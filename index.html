Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pricing, prep, scheduling, and timelines.

How do you price a painting job?

Pricing is based on scope (rooms/areas), surface condition, prep required, paint type/finish, and access. The free quote form helps us respond quickly.

Do you provide the paint?

Yes — we can supply paint, or we can use your preferred brand. We’ll recommend the right product for durability and finish.

How soon can you start?

It depends on current scheduling and project size. Small jobs can often be scheduled sooner. Submit a request and we’ll give you options.

What prep do you do before painting?

Prep may include patching, sanding, caulk, stain-blocking primer, masking, and protecting floors/furniture. Prep is what makes the finish look professional.

How long does a typical interior paint job take?

Many single-room jobs can be completed in a day. Larger projects (multiple rooms/full home) may take several days depending on repairs and drying times.

Do I need to move furniture?

We recommend clearing small items and valuables. We can help move light furniture and we protect remaining items with drop cloths/plastic as needed.
