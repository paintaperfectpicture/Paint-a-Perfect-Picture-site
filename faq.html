Quotes & Scheduling

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Estimates are free. For the fastest quote, text photos and a short description of the project (rooms, repairs, timeline).

How soon can you start?

It depends on current bookings and scope. We’ll give you realistic availability and options.

What information helps you quote accurately?

Photos, the number of rooms, ceiling height, if trim/doors are included, and any repairs (holes, cracks, water stains).

Do you require a deposit?

For most projects, we may request a deposit to reserve dates and secure materials. The amount depends on project size and scope.

Prep, Quality & Materials

What prep work is included?

We protect floors and furniture, patch minor holes, sand rough areas, caulk gaps when needed, and prime problem areas before painting.

Do you repair drywall before painting?

Yes. Drywall patching and prep are common parts of a quality paint job. Larger repairs can be scoped separately.

What paint brands do you use?

We recommend durable products based on surface and wear. If you prefer a specific brand, we can work with it.

What finish/sheens do you recommend?

Most walls are eggshell/satin, trim is semi-gloss, and ceilings are flat. We’ll recommend the best option for your space.

Do you guarantee your work?

We stand behind our workmanship. Warranty terms can depend on the surface condition and product used — we’ll clarify per job.

Residential Projects

How long does a room take to paint?

Many rooms take 1–2 days including prep and drying time. Multi-room projects are scheduled efficiently.

Do I need to move furniture?

If possible, move small items and valuables. We can help shift larger items and cover/protect what stays in the room.

Can you paint cabinets?

Yes. Cabinet painting requires extra prep and durable products. Send photos and we’ll scope it correctly.

Commercial / Industrial

Can you paint after hours or on weekends?

Yes — depending on scope and scheduling. We aim to minimize downtime for businesses.

Do you handle large spaces like warehouses?

Yes. Industrial work is quoted based on access, surface condition, coatings required, and facility workflow.

How do you keep job sites safe and clean?

We follow clear jobsite standards: protection, labeling, controlled work zones, and cleanup with a final walkthrough.
